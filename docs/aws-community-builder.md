# AWS Community Builder Program

The **AWS Community Builders program** is a global initiative by AWS to support and recognize passionate technical enthusiasts who actively share knowledge and contribute to the AWS community. The program provides resources, training, and networking opportunities to help members grow their expertise and amplify their impact.

---

## What Is the Program?

AWS Community Builders is a **free, year-long program** (renewable) designed for technical content creators, developers, and community contributors who:

- Build and learn on AWS
- Share knowledge through blogs, videos, talks, open-source projects, or social media
- Actively contribute to technical communities

Members gain exclusive access to AWS experts, service roadmap insights, certification vouchers, AWS credits, and a global network of like-minded builders.

---

## Program Categories

When applying, you select a specialty category aligned with your existing contributions:

| Category | Focus Areas |
|----------|-------------|
| **Serverless** | Lambda, API Gateway, DynamoDB, EventBridge, Step Functions |
| **Containers** | ECS, EKS, Fargate, Docker, Kubernetes on AWS |
| **Machine Learning & AI** | SageMaker, Bedrock, Rekognition, Comprehend, AI/ML services |
| **Security & Identity** | IAM, KMS, GuardDuty, WAF, Shield, Security Hub |
| **Data** | Databases (RDS, DynamoDB, Aurora), Analytics (Athena, Redshift, Glue) |
| **Developer Tools** | CodeSuite, CDK, CloudFormation, CLI, Amplify |
| **Front-End Web and Mobile** | Amplify, AppSync, Cognito, hosting |
| **Cloud Operations** | CloudWatch, Systems Manager, Config, Auto Scaling |
| **Networking & Content Delivery** | VPC, CloudFront, Route 53, Direct Connect, Load Balancing |
| **Storage** | S3, EBS, EFS, Storage Gateway, Backup |

!!! tip "Choose your category wisely"
    Pick the category that reflects the content you've **already created**, not the one you plan to explore. Your submitted examples must align with your chosen category.

## Application Process

### Timeline (2026)

| Event | Date |
|-------|------|
| Application window opens | Early January |
| Application deadline | ~Mid-January (typically 2-week window) |
| Review period | January – February |
| Acceptances sent | March |
| Program start | April |

!!! warning "Missed the window?"
    Applications open once per year. If you miss the window, you'll need to wait until the following year. [Join the waitlist](https://aws.amazon.com/developer/community/community-builders/) to be notified.

### How to Apply

1. **Prepare your content** — Gather 3 examples of your best AWS-related content (blog posts, videos, talks, open-source repos, social media threads)
2. **Choose your category** — Select the category that matches your existing contributions
3. **Write your application** — Use the STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result) to describe your impact
4. **Submit** — Complete the application form before the deadline

### Tips for a Strong Application

- **Quality over quantity** — Submit your 3 best pieces of content, not everything you've created
- **Show impact** — Explain how your content helped others (views, engagement, testimonials)
- **Be specific** — Demonstrate depth in your chosen category with concrete examples
- **Tell your story** — Share who you are, why you contribute, and your passion for helping others
- **Proofread** — Typos and unclear writing hurt your chances
- **Start early** — Begin preparing weeks before applications open

## Selection Criteria

AWS evaluates applicants based on:

| Criteria | What They Look For |
|----------|-------------------|
| **Content Quality** | Accuracy, clarity, production value, technical depth |
| **Consistency** | Regular contributions over time (especially in the past year) |
| **Impact** | Reach, engagement, testimonials, community feedback |
| **Category Alignment** | Content matches the chosen specialty category |
| **Community Spirit** | Genuine desire to help others learn and grow |

## FAQs

### Who can apply?

The program is open to anyone **18+** who creates technical content about AWS. You don't need to be an expert — passion and active contribution matter more.

### Do I need to be a senior professional?

No. The program welcomes contributors of all experience levels. Many Community Builders joined early in their careers.

### Can I re-apply if rejected?

Yes. If you're not accepted, review the feedback, continue creating content, and re-apply the following year.

### Is the program free?

Yes — there is no cost to join. AWS Community Builders is entirely free for members.

### How long does membership last?

Membership is for **one year** and can be renewed annually based on continued participation and contributions.

### Can I change my category later?

Category changes are generally not permitted mid-cycle. Choose carefully when applying.

### What happens if I don't participate?

The program expects active participation. Inactive members may not have their membership renewed.
