# How Long Do Exam Results Take?

!!! info "Key Fact"
    With the exception of Cloud Practitioner, you **do not** receive results immediately after the exam. Results are calculated and an anti-cheat process runs behind the scenes. This applies to both remote and test center exams.

## FAQ

### How long do most people wait?

Most candidates receive their results within **5 hours to 1 day**. Occasionally it takes longer, and sometimes it arrives much sooner.

### Does it take longer on weekends?

There are anecdotal reports of longer wait times on weekends, though this is not officially documented.

### What is the official deadline?

AWS states results are posted within **5 business days** (excluding holidays and weekends).

### Which arrives first — the Credly badge or the AWS pass email?

It varies. Sometimes the Credly badge arrives first, sometimes the AWS pass email. There is no consistent pattern.

### Does refreshing CertMetrics help?

No. Smashing refresh on CertMetrics does not make results arrive faster.

### What helps pass the time?

Going for a walk or sleeping seems to help it arrive faster.

### My exam still shows as "Upcoming Appointments" in CertMetrics — is this normal?

Yes. The exam will continue to show as an upcoming appointment until results are posted.
