## Programs for Women

### AWS re/Start Tech Training for Women

A cohort-based program delivered through local collaborating organizations that prepares women for entry to mid-level cloud roles.

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **Duration** | 20 weeks (full-time) |
| **Cost** | Free |
| **Curriculum** | AWS re/Start standard curriculum + professional skills |
| **Certification** | AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner |
| **Outcomes** | Industry mentors, employer connections, job placement |

[Official AWS re/Start Page](https://aws.amazon.com/training/restart/)

### Women in Cloud (Brazil)

Part of the Skills to Jobs Tech Alliance in Brazil, focused on inspiring, training, and connecting women with cloud careers.

- 2,800+ women impacted
- 1,100+ trained in cloud computing
- Job fairs and internship opportunities

### Amazon rekindle (India)

A return-to-work program for women who have taken a **12+ month career break**.

- Mentorship and on-the-job learning
- Orientation program for smooth transition
- Open to tech and non-tech roles

[Amazon rekindle](https://amazon.jobs/content/en/career-programs/rekindle)

### Amazon WoW

A networking group for women in tech offering workshops, career resources, and connections with Amazon recruiters.

| **Content** | Cloud fundamentals to AI/ML, career pathways |

### AWS Academy

Free, ready-to-teach cloud computing curriculum for higher education institutions.

- Designed for colleges and universities
- Students get free Skill Builder subscription (12 months)
- 100% discount on AWS Certification exams
- Access to the Emerging Talent Community
- Covers foundational to associate-level content

[AWS Academy](https://aws.amazon.com/training/awsacademy/)

Free AI training program powered by AWS Skill Builder and delivered in collaboration with Udacity.

| Phase | Duration | Details |
|-------|----------|---------|

| Udacity Nanodegree | August 4 – November 4, 2026 | Top 4,500 performers get fully-funded Nanodegree |

- 100,000 learners sponsored in 2026
- 170+ countries represented
- No prior experience required
- Graduates receive 3-month Skill Builder subscription

[AWS AI & ML Scholars](https://aws.amazon.com/about-aws/our-impact/scholars/)

### AWS Educate Job Board

Connects students (18+) with thousands of cloud-related jobs and internships from Amazon and AWS Partners worldwide.

## Workforce Development Programs

### AWS re/Start

A free, cohort-based workforce development program that prepares unemployed or underemployed individuals for cloud careers.

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **Cost** | Free to learner |
| **Duration** | 12–13 weeks (full-time) |
| **Curriculum** | Linux, Python, networking, security, AWS core services |
| **Certification** | AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner (exam fees covered) |
| **Outcomes** | 90%+ of graduates connected to interviews |
| **Eligibility** | 18+, unemployed or underemployed, no tech background needed |

**Two tracks:**

- **re/Start Foundation** — for individuals with little to no tech experience
- **re/Start Associate** — for those with non-cloud IT experience

[AWS re/Start](https://aws.amazon.com/training/restart/)

### AWS Skills to Jobs Tech Alliance

A coalition of employers, educational institutions, and government agencies working to close the tech skills gap.

**How It Works**

- Employers ("talent shapers") collaborate with educators to design curricula
- Students gain hands-on experience with AWS services
- Graduates get connected to entry-level cloud roles (Cloud Support Associate, Software Developer, Data Analyst)

**Active Regions (United States)**

| State | Focus |
|-------|-------|
| New York | City University of New York (CUNY) system, NYC employers |
| Illinois | Chicago-area institutions |
| Washington | Seattle Colleges |
| West Virginia | Rural workforce development |
| Texas | Houston, Dallas partnerships |
| Tennessee | Nashville-area institutions |

**Global Presence:** 9 countries — US, Egypt, Spain, Singapore, Italy, Germany, India, Colombia, Brazil

**Results:** 23,000+ learners connected with 350+ employers, 1,050+ educational institutions engaged, 250,000+ learners reached worldwide

[Skills to Jobs Tech Alliance — US](https://aws.amazon.com/government-education/skills-to-jobs-tech-alliance/united-states/)
[Skills to Jobs Tech Alliance — Global](https://aws.amazon.com/government-education/skills-to-jobs-tech-alliance/)

### AWS Emerging Talent Community

An online community for graduates of AWS training programs providing networking, resources, and job opportunities.

[AWS Emerging Talent Community](https://aws.amazon.com/training/emerging-talent-community/)
