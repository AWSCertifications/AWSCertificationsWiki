## Recommended Pathway

1. Read the [Exam Guide](https://d1.awsstatic.com/training-and-certification/docs-ai-practitioner/AWS-Certified-AI-Practitioner_Exam-Guide.pdf)
2. Do **one** video course
3. Do **one** set of quality practice exams (not dumps)
4. Optional — Complete [Cloud Quest: Generative AI](https://skillbuilder.aws/learn/5YB3FCEE1H/aws-cloud-quest-generative-ai-practitioner/26A81MG83V) for a free badge
5. Optional — Play the [Card Clash: Generative AI Game](https://skillbuilder.aws/learn/7V665TRGMY/aws-card-clash--an-architecture-design-game/7RJQ9341Z8)
6. Check for [vouchers and discounts](../vouchers-discounts.md)
7. Take and pass the exam!

## Exam Details

- **Exam code:** AIF-C01
- **Official page:** [AWS Certified AI Practitioner](https://aws.amazon.com/certification/certified-ai-practitioner/)
- **Exam guide:** [AIF-C01 Exam Guide (PDF)](https://d1.awsstatic.com/training-and-certification/docs-ai-practitioner/AWS-Certified-AI-Practitioner_Exam-Guide.pdf)
- **Number of questions:** 65
- **Results:** You will **not** receive a pass/fail at the end of the exam. Results are typically available within **5 business days**.

!!! warning "Question Types"
    This exam includes new question formats beyond standard multiple choice. See the exam guide for details.

## FAQ

**Where can I find vouchers or discounts?**
See [Vouchers & Discounts](../vouchers-discounts.md).

**When will I get my exam results?**
Unlike Cloud Practitioner, you will **not** get a pass/fail at the end. Results are typically available within 5 business days.

**Does this exam format differ from Cloud Practitioner?**
Yes. There are new question types beyond standard multiple choice.

**How many questions are on the exam?**
65 questions.

**Can someone with no AI experience pass this exam?**
Yes — this is a foundational exam. Zero experience is acceptable.

**Should I take Cloud Practitioner first?**
Not required. You can start directly with AI Practitioner.

**Does passing AIF renew my Cloud Practitioner?**
**No.** AIF will not renew CLF-CCP. Use the [Cloud Quest Recertify](https://explore.skillbuilder.aws/learn/course/external/view/elearning/17623/aws-cloud-quest-recertify-cloud-practitioner) game instead — it is free.
