## tl;dr

1. Get **one** video course and watch it end to end
2. Study **CAF** and **WAF** in detail
3. Do decent **practice exams** (not dumps)
4. Take and pass the exam!

## FAQ

**Do I need all this material?**
No. One from each category is enough. Example: free YouTube course + TutorialsDojo = pass.

**Do I need hands-on work?**
Recommended but optional at this level.

**Where can I find vouchers?**
See [Vouchers & Discounts](../vouchers-discounts.md).

**Can I cheat using dumps?**
You can, but you risk a ban. Not worth it.

**Can I pass with only free resources?**
Possible, but spending on decent practice exams is strongly recommended. If you cannot afford the exam, pursue free digital badges (Cloud Essentials, Cloud Quest) as alternatives.

**Can I skip CCP and go straight to Associate?**
Absolutely. If you are aiming higher than foundational, go directly to Associate level.

**Can someone new to IT take this exam?**
Yes — it is designed for beginners.

**Is it worth it?**
That depends on your goals. Search the community for discussion threads.

**Is coding required?**
No — this is a beginner-level exam with no coding involved.
