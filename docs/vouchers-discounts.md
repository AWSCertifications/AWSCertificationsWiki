### Retake Protection Option

When purchasing via Udemy, you can pay approximately **10% more** for a **retake option** — if you fail your first attempt, you get a second attempt at no additional cost.

**How it works:**

1. Choose the "Exam Voucher + Retake" option at checkout
2. If you fail your first attempt, receive a free retake
3. If you pass on the first attempt, you still get the [50% Exam Pass Benefit](exam-benefits.md) for your next exam

!!! tip
    Buy the voucher closer to your exam date — vouchers are non-refundable and you cannot afford to let them expire.

**Retake policy:** You must wait **14 calendar days** before retaking a failed exam ([AWS retake policy](https://aws.amazon.com/certification/faqs/)).

## Recurring Benefits

### AWS Exam Pass Benefit

Pass **any** AWS certification exam and receive a **50% discount** on your next AWS certification exam.

**FAQ:**

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| Does this apply to any AWS exam? | Yes — any exam you pass qualifies |
| What if I fail? | No benefit if you fail |
| Where do I find my voucher? | Go to [aws.training](https://aws.training) → Certification → Sign in → Expand "BENEFITS" → Certification benefits |
| When does the voucher expire? | **3 years** — as long as your certification is active |
| Does a BETA exam count? | Yes |
| Can I use a Practitioner voucher for a Professional exam? | Yes — valid for any AWS exam |
| Can I stack it with another discount? | No — only one voucher per exam booking |
| Can I sell or transfer it? | **No.** Benefits are non-transferable. Violating terms may invalidate your exam results |
| Can I re-use it if I fail? | No — single use only |
| Can I re-use it if I cancel the exam? | Yes — as long as the benefit has not expired |
| What if my certification expires? | The benefit expires with it |

See also: [Exam Benefits](exam-benefits.md)

### AWS Partner Network — Certification Readiness (APCR)

If your employer is an **AWS Partner**, you may qualify for a discounted certification exam voucher through the **AWS Partner Certification Readiness (APCR)** program.

**Eligibility period:** 1 January 2026 – 31 December 2026

**Requirements:**

1. Register for an APCR program
2. Complete more than **50%** of the assigned self-paced digital learning plan
3. Attend more than **1 live session** of the APCR program

Limited to **one voucher per participant**. Terms and conditions apply.

[Register for APCR](https://aws.amazon.com/partners/training/certification/)

[APCR Terms and Conditions](https://pages.awscloud.com/GLOBAL_TRAINCERT_LN_Terms-and-conditions-APCR.html)

[Find AWS Partners](https://partners.amazonaws.com/)

!!! tip
    If your employer is an AWS Partner, check internally for certification assistance programs — many Partners sponsor certification attempts for employees.
